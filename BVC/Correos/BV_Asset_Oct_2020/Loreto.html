Description Deluxe Studio

Our residential-style Deluxe Suite is larger than the size of a standard hotel room at 645-square-feet of spacious luxury. Designed with the modern day traveler in mind, these suites have everything you need for a vacation in paradise. The room includes two queen-size beds, a spacious bathroom, an LCD TV, and a kitchenette complete with a two-burner stove, mini-fridge, coffee maker, blender, toaster, and a sufficient amount of dishes for everyone staying in the room! Every Deluxe Suite at Villa del Palmar at the Islands of Loreto has a large balcony that offers breathtaking views of the Sierra de la Giganta Mountain Range, TPC Danzante Bay, or the resort’s tropical gardens and pool deck. Just imagine waking up to the golden rays cresting over the stunning Loreto hotel property each and every day throughout your stay.
