Loreto

A magical town considered to be the first human settlement in Baja California Sur. Experience an isolated village where extraordinary paintings and petroglyphs can be enjoyed surrounded by crystal clear waters.

Wonders

Loreto is impressive and seems to be a place where only the luckiest live. A place filled with history and traditions and surrounded by the uniqueness of its beaches.

Experiences

Enveloped by The Sea of Cortez and the Sierra de la Giganta there exist an impressive amount of activities to choose from such as kayaking, diving, hiking, fishing, and you may also see whales.

Golf

Be challenged by an 18-hole masterpiece that features the mesmerizing views of Loreto Bay. Featuring landscapes such as valleys, dunes, foothills, among much more.
