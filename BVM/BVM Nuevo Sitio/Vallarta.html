Puerto Vallarta

If your idea of paradise is a tropical beach with the most beautiful sunsets and distinctive nightlife Puerto Vallarta should be on your
checklist.

From our emerald waters of the Bay of Banderas to the luscious greenery of the Sierra Madre Mountains, Puerto Vallarta is your optimum
Mexican destination where Mexico truly comes alive! Puerto Vallarta’s old-world charm with all the modern comforts will marvel you and
meet all your vacation desires.

Puerto Vallarta has something for everyone, watch the Dolphins playfully jump and swim in the Banderas Bay, enjoy a romantic candlelit
dinner for two with breathtaking views, take a fun-filled Pirate themed cruise suited for the whole family, or simply join us on our famous
Malecón (boardwalk) to see this city in all its glory. Swing through the jungle canopy, parasail over Los Muertos Beach, or stroll through Old
Town and discover why Vallarta has been a vacation destination for more than 50 years.

What to do in Vallarta?

Bring the family!... Or not. Puerto Vallarta is your perfect destination when it comes to the best of both worlds. We have some of the best adventures, trek through our tropical rainforest-like mountains on your personal ATV, repel from beautiful waterfalls, check out our gorgeous hidden beaches, parasail over the Bay of Banderas and if you are in luck you will see whales or dolphins! Your evening stroll will consist of soaking in our Boardwalk known as the Malecon. Against the backdrop of the most beautiful sunsets, you will enjoy the music and visible culture all around you. Check out available tours at www.myexperiencevallarta.com

Water/Season

Summer

Between July and September, you will enjoy a hot season with a lot of sun and rain. The temperature this season usually is between 73° and 92.5°.

Fall

Between October and December, you will enjoy the tropical heat without it being overwhelming. The temperature this season usually is between 64.4° and 92.5°.

Winter

Between December and February, you can enjoy a template climate ideal for escaping the snow. The temperature this season usually is between 61.3° and 85.8°.

Spring

Between March and June, you will enjoy windy and sunny vacations with great temperatures. The temperature this season usually is between 62.4° and 87.8°.
