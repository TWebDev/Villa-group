Cancun

Recognized throughout the world for its spectacular white sand beaches, crystal turquoise blue water, deep jungles, and unique cultural
destination.

Cancun offers pure paradise and an array of magical, spiritual, and extreme adventures. Enjoy the Mayan culture, visit the surreal sight of
the Mayan Ruins and enjoy night shows in these ruins, dive into the mysterious natural cenotes, shop the handicraft markets, relax by the
beautiful waters and wait for the sun to come down, and to close a unique experience visit the incomparable Mexican nightlife to come
alive once again. Cancun has it all and is waiting for you!

What to do in Cancun?

Where are the party people?? This is your destination; for all of you looking for the greatest party of the year don't miss Cancun. Get swept up in the most beautiful beaches with the bluest water and the white, soft sand. Imagine your lazy day sipping cocktails and then putting on your finest for the evening's best parties. Looking for adventure? Cancun also offers some great activities, visit Tulum and soak in the Mayan culture of the ruins, take a day trip to Isla Mujeres and enjoy one of the finest beaches, don't forget the natural cenotes, the snorkeling, the natural cavern adventures, or the adventure parks! Water/Season

Summer

Between July and October, you will enjoy a hot season with a lot of sun and rain. The temperature this season usually is between 76° and 89°.

Fall

Between October and December, you will enjoy the tropical heat without it being overwhelming. The temperature this season usually is between 68° and 81°.

Winter

Between December and February, you can enjoy a template climate ideal for escaping the snow. The temperature this season usually is between 68º and 80°.

Spring

Between March and June, you will enjoy windy and sunny vacations with great temperatures. The temperature this season usually is between 71° and 86°.
