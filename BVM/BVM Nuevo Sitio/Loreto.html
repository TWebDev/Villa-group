Loreto

Come Visit the Magical Islands of Loreto and truly have the most relaxing and peaceful vacation of all time while being pampered like a
celebrity!

In the Sea of Cortez in Mexico lies an amazing jewel is known as Loreto, a colonial city 300 years old, where five islands surround an
ecological reserve, virtually untouched. Loreto is a little-known Mexican resort town, offering quiet beaches, scenic views, rich history, and
stunning natural habitats.

It’s the kind of place for eco-travelers, who love to explore the beauty of Mother Nature. If you want to experience the unique combination
of desert landscape, mountains, and sea, Loreto is like no other place you have visited. Take a day trip through the past with a visit to the
historic San Javier Mission, or a boat tour to Coronado Island, a paradise for divers, snorkelers, and kayakers.

Come and discover for yourself an untouched paradise in Baja California Sur!

What to do in Loreto?

Looking for activity and adventure or just to relax? The list goes on and on – this is a couple's haven. Come celebrate your anniversary in our amazing spa, enjoy tropical drinks by our beautiful pools, or enjoy the tranquil beach where you will be guaranteed to marvel in the natural preservation of the sea life. For the ones craving an action-packed vacation – Snorkel, dive, mountain bike, ATV, go on an adventure to other islands, or check out our Danzante Bay Golf Course. Water/Season

Summer

Between July and October, you will enjoy a hot season with a lot of sun and rain. The temperature this season usually is between 73° and 93°.

Fall

Between October and December, you will enjoy the tropical heat without it being overwhelming. The temperature this season usually is between 58° and 90°.

Winter

Between December and February, you can enjoy a template climate ideal for escaping the snow. The temperature this season usually is between 55° and 80°.

Spring

Between March and June, you will enjoy windy and sunny vacations with great temperatures. The temperature this season usually is between 57° and 87°.
