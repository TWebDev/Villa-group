PUERTO VALLARTA

From our emerald waters of the Bay of Banderas to the Sierra Madre Mountains' luscious greenery,
Puerto Vallarta is your optimum Mexican destination where Mexico truly comes alive!
Puerto Vallarta's old-world charm will marvel you and meet all your vacation desires with all the modern comforts.
Puerto Vallarta has something for everyone, watch the dolphins playfully jump and swim in the Banderas Bay,
enjoy a romantic candlelit dinner for two with breathtaking views, take a fun-filled Pirate themed cruise suited for the whole family,
or simply join us on our famous Malecon (boardwalk) to see this city in all its glory. Swing through the jungle canopy,
parasail over Los Muertos Beach, or stroll through Old Town and discover why Vallarta has been a vacation destination for more than 50 years.
