Cabo San Lucas has become one of the most prominent vacations and spa destinations in Mexico, home to famous El Arco de Cabo San
Lucas, a legendary landmark and a hotspot for all ages. Located at the very southern tip of the Baja Peninsula, the La Laguna Mountains
provide the scenic backdrop for the gorgeous sandy beaches along the coastline. Known as a premier destination for outdoor sports and
recreation, Cabo San Lucas and the Los Cabos area is home to world-class golfing, fishing, snorkeling, and surfing. Here you have it all -
everything from sunset sailing, rounds of early morning golf, exhilarating spa treatments to partying to the wee hours of the morning at
some of the most prestigious local nightspots.

A true fishermen’s dream location offering one of the largest jackpots paid fishing tournaments every year in October known as the
Bisbee’s Black and Blue Marlin Fishing Tournament. Also, every winter sea life lovers can observe pods of whales that migrate to the warm
waters of the Gulf to bear their calves.

Check out Cabo San Lucas definitely not a spot to miss!

What to do in Cabo San Lucas?

If you have ever seen any pictures of Cabo you will recognize El Arco where the Pacific Ocean meets the Sea of Cortez, explore this area by Glass bottom boat or snorkeling, this area is also referred to as Lands End. Cabo is known for its fishing and holds one of the largest fishing tournaments every year in October known as Bisbee's Fishing Tournament. With its desert-like backdrop, the beaches with their turquoise waters stand out even more and will make your desire to jump in irresistible. Cruise the Marina boardwalk and enjoy some of the finest food, then partake in a night of bar and club hopping all in one intersection!! For you party lovers this is your spot!
