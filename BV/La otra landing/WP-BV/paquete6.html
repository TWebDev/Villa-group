Bluebay Grand Esmeralda is an All Inclusive beach hotel that offers two swimming pools, an oceanfront hot tub and a variety of family friendly activities. This resort is conveniently located in the hotel zone of Mayan Rivera and is also close to the downtown area and boardwalk.

The city of Mayan Rivera is famous for its lovely surroundings, stunning views of the ocean and friendly people. Explore all the beautiful spots the city has to offer, such as, the historic center, the handicrafts market and the lovely colonial cathedral. Enjoy wonderful and conveniently located facilities,including a panoramic Jacuzzi, two swimming pools, sports bar and massage center. You will enjoy a pleasant and fun vacation in this lovely city, famous for its year round good weather. Bluebay Grand Esmeralda is the perfect place to spend a memorable vacation.
